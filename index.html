Overview


Growing urbanisation, rising vehicle ownership, globalisation of economies and shortage of resources has posed major challenges to the transportation sector. Mobility, accessibility and safety continue to be daunting challenges to transportation professionals’ world over. In this context, cutting edge research that could provide sustainable solutions to transport related problems is the need of the hour. Conference on Transportation Systems Engineering and Management (CTSEM) aims to bring together researchers, scientists, engineers, research scholars and post graduate students in different areas of Transportation Engineering and Management. Earlier CTSEMs were organised in 2014 at NIT Calicut, 2015 at NIT Tiruchirapalli, 2016 at RV College of Engineering, Bangalore and 2017 at Anna University, Chennai, 2018 at NIT Warangal, 2019 at SVNIT, Surat, 2020 at NATPAC and CoE, Trivandrum and 2021 at NIT Calicut. The objectives of this 9th CTSEM are to provide an opportunity for the post graduate students, research scholars and transportation professionals to present their work and network with the peers; learn about advancements and applications in their fields; minimise the duplication of research efforts and arrive at future directions for research.
9th CTSEM will cover the conceptual aspects related to transport planning, traffic engineering and pavement technology.


Call for Papers


Papers are invited on the topics outlined and others falling within the scope of the event. Papers are to be prepared as per the ASCE conference proceedings paper format. Acceptance of the papers will be based on peer review results and if accepted for publication, must be presented at the event.
Final acceptance will be based on the full-length paper, which if acceptedfor publication, must be presented at the conference. The language of the conferencewill be English. Negotiations with leading publishers are underway to publish someofthe selected high quality papers presented in the conference in the form of proceedings.
Select papers from the conference will be published by Springer as a proceedings book volume(Scopus Indexed). Springer will conduct quality checks on the accepted papers and only papers that pass these checks will be published.
Springer Nature does not charge any money for publication of Non-Open Access content. Abstracts/extended abstracts and short papers (less than 4 pages) are not considered for publication.
All the papers are to be submitted online at: https://easychair.org/account/signin?l=3GeDsh24U3WCfx7pL0xggW

  • Pavement Materials and Characterisation

  • Road Asset Management
  • Sustainable Transportation/NMT
  • Traffic Crashes and Safety
  • Traffic Management and TDM
  • Traffic Modelling and Management
  • Transit Oriented Development
  • Transportation Economics and Policy
  • Transportation Infrastructure Financing
  • Travel Behaviour Analysis
  • Travel Demand Forecasting
  • Urban Mobility

Steps to Upload the paper

  1. Select upload paper option from navbar
  2. Enter the title of the paper
  3. Upload the paper in pdf format only as per the paper template which is available at important links of this website. No other formats are allowed
  4. A confirmation mail stating the tracking id will be sent to you

Peer review process

  1. The uploaded papers will be checked for similarity index
  2. If it satisfy the required similarity index then the papers are sent to reviewers for peer review process.
  3. The reviewers' recommendations determine whether a paper will be accepted for publication / accepted subject to change / subject to resubmission with significant changes / accepted for oral presentation / rejected
  4. The recommendations of the reviewer will uploaded onto the website and the authors can view and do incorporate the corrections, if any.
  5. For papers which require changes, the same reviewers will be used to ensure that the quality of the revised paper is acceptable.

"Final Submission and Payment of Registration fee"

  2. The payment of the registration fee is through NEFT (or in worst case DD). The detials of online payment are given on the conference webiste
  3. Select "upload payment Receipt" (Scanned copy of the online payment receipt or DD) option from the navbar at top
  4. Upload picture of ID proof and the payment receipt
  5. Then from the same upload paper option, go to "final paper upload"
  6. Upload Copyright Form (which is available at important links page)
